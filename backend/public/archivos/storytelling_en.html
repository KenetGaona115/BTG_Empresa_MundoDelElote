Storytelling

Mundo del elote

Once upon a time there were 3 companions who were in the biological chemist 1, at the beginning they were not very close why they were not in the same group from the beginning of their high school, they never thought that they would end up being a team to form a micro-enterprise.

Thanks to the communication they created, they were able to agree from the beginning with the ideas that they had to do the microenterprise, the winning idea came from a colleague who really likes the corn, but his intention was that each person who tasted his products fulfilled their cravings, from there came the name "El mundo del elote" since the objective was to offer different products derived from corn, which turned out to be a successful micro business, our first sale was chaos!! We were not prepared for so much demand in our product and more because the time we had was short for sale, the first thing we sold was a jar of corn, see our first $23.00 pesos in the box was one of the best feelings, that after so much effort and dedication was having results, that's how we needed corn because of so much demand.
